About Me

Hi, I’m Harry Polisana, a versatile and passionate professional from South Africa. I am a Full Stack Developer, writer, and a Retail Business Management student at Tshwane University of Technology. I combine technical skills, creativity, and business knowledge to develop meaningful applications, craft compelling stories, and understand business operations at a deeper level. As a developer, I have created numerous projects including:

  • Civic Contribution App – Encourages community engagement and volunteering.
  • Purposeful AI Agent – A smart assistant for productivity and decision-making.
  • Expense Tracker – Manage finances with ease and transparency.
  • City Zoo App – Explore zoo animals, habitats, and conservation info.
  • Calculator – Responsive, easy-to-use web calculator for quick calculations.
Beyond development, I am a published writer with works that explore emotional healing, environmentalism, and personal transformation. My literary works include short stories and research studies that have been recognized at conferences. My experience at the FNB App Academy has sharpened my coding, project management, and collaboration skills, allowing me to transform ideas into fully functional web applications. I am ambitious, disciplined, and constantly learning new technologies to stay ahead in software development while merging creativity and problem-solving in every project. Whether coding, writing, or analyzing business trends, my goal is to create value and inspire others through my work.
